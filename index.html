Who is Gary?

A proper local tradesman
you can actually trust

I started operating diggers when I was barely out of school — that's over 40 years ago now. I've done it all: drains, foundations, demolition, clearance, driveways, fencing, gritting icy roads at 5am. Over 10,000 jobs across Surrey and beyond.

What sets me apart isn't the machinery — it's the attitude. I answer my phone. I show up on time. I explain what I'm doing and why. If I spot something that needs doing while I'm there — I'll mention it, and often just sort it out.

Most of my work comes from neighbours recommending me to neighbours. That tells you everything. When someone calls me because their friend said "get Gary" — I take that seriously every single time.

  • I always answer my phone — or call you straight back within the hour
  • No hidden costs — you'll know the full price before I lift a finger
  • I'll help out with small extras that aren't "part of the job"
  • I leave your site clean and tidy every single day, without being asked
  • I cover all of Surrey and will happily travel further if you need me
  • No job is too small — I genuinely mean that

What Gary Can Do For You

Services — big and small

🚜

Excavation & Digging

Trenches, foundations, drainage runs, pond digging, and general earthworks. Compact machinery for tight gardens — Gary gets into spots others simply can't reach.

✦ Happy to dig by hand for really tight spaces

🏚️

Demolition

Garages, sheds, outbuildings, old walls, patios, concrete. Safe, properly assessed, and fully cleared away. Gary leaves things tidier than he found them — always.

✦ Rubble disposal included — no skips for you to organise

🧹

Clearance

Land clearance, overgrown gardens, site clearance after build work. No mess left behind. If there's something you want to keep, Gary will set it aside without being asked.

✦ Happy to sort what's rubbish and what's not

🚛

Building Material Transport

Aggregate, soil, hardcore, sand, rubble — collected or delivered. Tipper trailer on hand. Gary can even source materials locally and save you the legwork.

✦ Saves you arranging separate deliveries

🔩

Fence Installation

Closeboard, post & rail, agricultural and security fencing. Machine-driven posts for a solid, lasting result in any ground. Gary advises on the best type for your situation for free.

✦ Free advice on materials and fence type

🧂

Road Gritting

Private roads, driveways, car parks, commercial yards — kept safe when it freezes. Gary offers early morning callouts so you can get out when you need to.

✦ There before you need to leave the house

🪨

Hardcore & Levelling

Spreading, compacting and levelling for driveways, yards, and building bases. Done once, done right — no corners cut, no sinking later.

✦ Proper compaction — no shortcuts

💧

Drainage & Ditching

French drains, soakaways, ditch clearing and re-profiling. Gary has seen every type of waterlogging problem over 40 years — and knows how to fix them properly.

✦ Free assessment of what's causing the problem

🌿

Topsoil & Turfing Prep

Strip and remove topsoil, or deliver and spread fresh topsoil ready for seeding or turfing. Gardens, paddocks, playing areas — Gary covers it all.

✦ Can recommend a good turfing contact too
