What I Offer

Services

🚜

Excavation & Digging

Trenches, foundations, drainage runs, pond digging, and general earthworks. Compact machinery to access tight spaces without sacrificing power.

🏚️

Demolition

Safe and efficient demolition of garages, outbuildings, sheds, walls, patios, and small structures. Fully assessed before work begins.

🧹

Clearance

Land clearance, garden clearance, site clearance, and overgrown plot reclamation. Leaving your land clean, level, and ready to use.

🚛

Building Material Transport

Collection and delivery of aggregate, soil, hardcore, sand, and rubble. Tipper trailer available for swift haulage to and from site.

🔩

Fence Installation

Post & rail, closeboard, agricultural fencing, and security fencing. Machine-driven posts for a solid, lasting result in any ground condition.

🧂

Road Gritting

Winter gritting service for private roads, driveways, car parks, and commercial yards. Keep your site safe and accessible through freezing conditions.

🪨

Hardcore & Levelling

Hardcore spreading and compaction, levelling uneven ground, and creating solid bases for driveways, parking areas, and outbuildings.

💧

Drainage & Ditching

French drains, soakaways, ditch clearing and re-profiling. Solve your waterlogging and surface water problems fast.

🌿

Topsoil & Turfing Prep

Strip and remove topsoil, or deliver and spread fresh topsoil ready for seeding or turfing. Gardens, paddocks, and sports areas covered.
